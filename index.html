John Cleese

Devilishly handsome and young John Cleese
Events
  • October 1939 – Born in Somerset, England, making the world a better place.
  • 1970 to 1973 – Rector at the University of St. Andrews
  • 1980 –Received the BAFTA Television Award for "Best Light Entertainment Performance" for Fawlty Towers
  • 1987 – Received an Emmy for Outstanding Guest Actor in Cheers, "Simon Says"
  • 1988 –Received the BAFTA Best Actor in a Leading Role for A Fish Called Wanda
  • 1999 to 2006 – Professor-at-Large, Cornell University
  • 2015 – Memoir: So Anyway ...
